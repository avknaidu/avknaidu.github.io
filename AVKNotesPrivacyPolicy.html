Privacy Policy

AVK Notes does not collect any personal information

Collection and use of personal data

Personal information will be requested only in cases where such information is necessary to identify you for the purpose of accessing some features of the App. Typically this is done in cases where it is necessary to cases such as syncing data between multiple devices. All information related to your account is transmitted directly to Appropriate third party services that you subscribe to.

Control and access to personal information

The personal information you provide to AVK Notes for Login is not shared with third any parties any time. All Information collected will be directly from 3rd Party API's. If you decide to revoke Access of AVK Notes with the 3rd party companies, you can do so by contacting the 3rd party vendor.

AVK Notes reserves the right to modify the privacy policy.
